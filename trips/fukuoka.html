August 3-6, 2025 · 4 Days

Fukuoka

Shrines, ramen, omakase, hot spring towns, and one very memorable train ride.

  1. Home
  2. ›
  3. Fukuoka
Destination Fukuoka
Dates Aug 3-6, 2025
Hotel Dormy Inn Hakata Gion
Highlights Dazaifu, sushi, Yufuin

This trip was a short but full visit to northern Kyushu. I stayed near Hakata Gion, spent a day around Dazaifu, explored shrines and food in Fukuoka, and took the Yufuin no Mori train out to Yufuin for a slower countryside day.

I wanted this page to work as both a memory log and a mini guide, so each day includes what I did plus short notes about the places and foods that made the trip special.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

  1. 01

    Dazaifu, Hakata Station, and Kiwamiya

    August 3 · Dazaifu and Hakata

    I started at 太宰府天満宮 (Dazaifu Tenmangu Shrine), walked the shrine approach, stopped at a local cafe for mocha and 梅ヶ枝餅 (Umegae Mochi), ate lunch at 一蘭 (Ichiran Ramen), visited 博多駅 (Hakata Station), and ended the day with dinner at 極味や.

  2. 02

    Shrines, Bakery, and Omakase

    August 4 · Hakata and Yakuin

    After breakfast at the hotel, I visited 櫛田神社 (Kushida-Jinja) and 注連懸稲荷神社 (Shimekake Inari Shrine). Then I went to dacomecca for bread and had dinner at 鮨志郎 (Sushi Shiro), an omakase restaurant booked through OMAKASE.

    Sushi Shiro reservation page

  3. 03

    Yufuin Day Trip

    August 5 · Yufuin

    I took the 由布院之森 (Yufuin no Mori) train to 由布院 (Yufuin), ate lunch at a local restaurant, walked around 金鱗湖 (Lake Kinrin), 湯布院フローラルヴィレッジ (Yufuin Floral Village), and 湯の坪街道 (Yunotsubo Street), then came back for dinner at Yakiniku Benjamin (燒肉垂榕).

  4. 04

    Airport Matcha Before Flying Home

    August 6 · Fukuoka Airport

    My flight was at noon, so the last stop was simple: matcha at the airport from Matcha Tokyo before heading home.

What Made These Stops Special

Dazaifu

Dazaifu Tenmangu

Dazaifu Tenmangu is dedicated to Sugawara Michizane, who is worshipped as Tenjin, the deity of learning, culture, and the arts. Many students visit to pray for academic success, and the shrine is strongly connected to plum blossoms.

Trip Highlights

  • 🍜

    Most Fukuoka Moment

    Eating Ichiran near Dazaifu after visiting the shrine made the lunch feel connected to the place, not just a random ramen stop.

  • 🚆

    Best Transportation

    The Yufuin no Mori train made the day trip feel special before I even arrived in Yufuin.

  • 🍣

    Best Food

    The omakase at Sushi Shiro was memorable because each course felt carefully prepared, from otoro and uni to the smaller seasonal details.
