February 27-March 4, 2026 · 6 Days

Las Vegas

Strip shows, late-night food, Zion hikes, and a road trip to Antelope Canyon and Horseshoe Bend.

  1. Home
  2. ›
  3. Las Vegas
Destination Las Vegas + Zion + Page
Dates Feb 27-Mar 4, 2026
Stay Southern Highlands Airbnb
Highlights Strip shows, Zion, Antelope Canyon

This trip mixed classic Las Vegas entertainment with two road-trip days outside the city. I had time for food stops, shows, the Strip, Fremont Street, Zion National Park, and Page, Arizona.

I kept the same structure as my Fukuoka page, so this one works as both a trip memory log and a mini guide. I will add the Vegas photos later, but the itinerary and special-stop notes are already organized.

Day-by-Day Itinerary

  1. 01

    Arrival, Tea Stops, and Korean Food

    February 27 · Las Vegas arrival day

    I checked in at 6479 Southern Pine Way, stopped by drink shops like Urban Matcha, Molly Tea, and CHICHA San Chen, then had dinner at Tang Tang Tang with Korean dishes like potato rib soup and cheesy spicy ribs with rice cake.

  2. 02

    Taiwanese Food and a Bellagio Show Night

    February 28 · Las Vegas Strip

    I started with Taiwan Deli and TP TEA, then went to the Coca-Cola Store on the Strip. At night I had dinner at Peter Luger Steak House at Caesars Palace, watched O by Cirque du Soleil, and ended the evening at the Fountains of Bellagio.

  3. 03

    Brunch, the Strip, and Night Shows

    March 1 · Las Vegas

    I had brunch at Sadelle's, spent time walking the Las Vegas Strip, visited The Venetian, and watched Jabbawockeez. Later I also stopped at Tacos El Gordo and Sphere.

  4. 04

    Zion Day Trip and Old Vegas

    March 2 · Zion and Fremont Street

    I picked up the rental car early at Harry Reid Airport, considered stopping by the Las Vegas sign, then drove to Zion National Park. The main stops were The Narrows and Watchman Trail. Back in Las Vegas, I went to East Fremont Street and Fremont Street Experience at night.

  5. 05

    Antelope Canyon and Page, Arizona

    March 3 · Page day trip

    I left around 5:30 in the morning for Lower Antelope Canyon. After that, I visited Page Shores Amphitheater, had food at New York Teriyaki, and continued to Horseshoe Bend, Glen Canyon Dam Overlook, Glen Canyon Dam, and Lake Powell. That night back in Las Vegas, I also stopped at Tacos El Gordo and Herbs and Rye.

  6. 06

    Final Food Stops and Check-Out

    March 4 · Last day in Las Vegas

    Before checking out, I planned a final mix of food and city stops including New Asian BBQ, CHICHA San Chen, and Bellagio Hotel & Casino before heading home.

What Made These Stops Special

Bellagio

O by Cirque du Soleil

O is one of the most iconic Las Vegas productions because it combines theater, acrobatics, and a full aquatic stage. It stood out on this trip as a classic Vegas show-night experience.

Trip Highlights

  • 🎭

    Most Vegas Moment

    Watching O and then walking to the Bellagio fountains felt like the most classic Las Vegas night of the trip.

  • 🚗

    Best Road Trip Day

    The Page, Arizona day was the biggest contrast to the Strip because it packed Lower Antelope Canyon, Horseshoe Bend, and Glen Canyon into one long drive.

  • 🌮

    Best Casual Food Stop

    Tacos El Gordo stood out as one of the most memorable casual food stops, especially after a full day of shows or driving.
